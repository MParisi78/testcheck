Enable the microphone

Safari needs your permission to use the microphone.

On iPhone / iPad:
1. Open the Settings app
2. Scroll down and tap Safari
3. Under "Settings for Websites" tap Microphone
4. Set to Allow (or find this site in the list and switch it on)
5. Come back here and tap the mic button again
If you added this to your Home Screen:
1. Open Settings → Apps → Cherokee CoPilot (or the app name you gave it)
2. Turn on Microphone
